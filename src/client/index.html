Triage Queue

What is this?

After you Scan Emails, the app suggests which messages to archive (remove from inbox), unsubscribe from, or keep for review. This queue lists those suggestions.

How to use it

  • Approve — Mark this suggestion as correct (e.g. “archive” or “archive + unsubscribe”). The email is labeled for deletion but not removed yet.
  • Reject — Dismiss the suggestion and keep the email as-is.
  • Approve Selected — Mark all checked items as approved at once.
  • Delete all auto-delete — Permanently archive every email you’ve approved for deletion. Run this when you’re ready to clear them from your inbox.
